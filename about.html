About THE YAQEEN SCHOOL


Recognizing the vital need for a modern, Akhirah-oriented educational institution in this age of advanced technology, THE YAQEEN SCHOOL was founded under the guidance of respected scholars and forward-thinking educators.

Alhamdulillah, through consistent dedication and collective efforts, THE YAQEEN SCHOOL has established itself as a reputable institution known for delivering quality education within an Islamic environment.

Our vision is rooted in creating a balanced harmony between strong Islamic values and the evolving needs of modern society. We are proud to have a team of dedicated management, passionate educators, and supportive parents working together with a shared vision for excellence and growth.


Vision


To earn the pleasure of Allah Almighty through meaningful education and character building.


Mission


  • To nurture future generations of the Muslim Ummah under the mentorship of scholars, educators, and professionals.
  • To provide academically sound, spiritually enriching education in an Islamic environment.

Aims and Values


At THE YAQEEN SCHOOL, we strive to work collaboratively with students and parents to fulfill our mission. Character and values are at the core of our educational journey. We follow a clear code of conduct and promote high moral standards in line with Islamic teachings.

Students are encouraged to be honest, respectful, compassionate, and responsible. Our curriculum is designed to be broad and balanced, connecting past knowledge to present-day experiences, while fostering independence in learning.

We maintain a stimulating learning environment through activity-based learning, enriching library programs, and the creative use of classroom resources.


Affiliation


Affiliated with Cambridge Assessment International Education.


Our Team


We are proud to have a dedicated team of educators and staff who are committed to providing the best possible education for our students. Our team is composed of experienced professionals who are passionate about teaching and nurturing young minds.
