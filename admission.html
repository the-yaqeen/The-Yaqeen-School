Admission Process

At THE YAQEEN SCHOOL, the admission process is designed to be simple and smooth for parents and students. Parents are requested to fill out the admission form and submit the required documents. Once the form is submitted, the student will be scheduled for an entry test.

The test helps us understand the child's current academic level. Admissions are granted based on merit and seat availability.

Admission Criteria / Eligibility

  • Admissions in all grades are based on merit and availability of seats.
  • For Nursery: A skill-based observation session will be conducted. No preparation is required.
  • For Prep-I to O-Level: Assessment will be taken in English, Urdu, and Mathematics.
  • From Grade-I onwards: A Quranic assessment will also be conducted.
